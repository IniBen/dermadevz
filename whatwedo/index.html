WHAT
WE
DO

DermaDevz connects patients and referring physicians to the leading dermatologists through a secure web and mobile platform for timely, affordable consultations and accurate and comprehensive diagnoses and treatment plans. DermaDevz overcomes the barriers – such as long wait times for an appointment, underserved areas or remote locations – that currently limit access to dermatologist consultations. DermaDevz services are covered by most health plans, and we offer an affordable self-pay option. We work with physicians, hospitals, employers, universities, health care plans and other partners to provide coordination and continuity of patient care.
